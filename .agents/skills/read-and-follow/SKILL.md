# Read And Follow

Read the referenced file or URL first. Treat it as the source of instructions for this turn.

Follow the explicit instructions, constraints, and stop conditions in the reference.

Do not infer extra work from the reference's filename, format, location, or surrounding workflow. If the scope or next action is unclear, summarize what you learned and ask one concise question.

Keep the response compact: what was read, what matters, and what happens next.
